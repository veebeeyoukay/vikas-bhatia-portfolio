## Market Positioning

### Target Geography
- **Primary:** South Tampa, Davis Islands, Hyde Park, Bayshore Beautiful
- **Why:** Affluent neighborhoods with high disposable income and appreciation for premium services

### Target Demographics
- Household income: $300,000+
- Home value: $750,000+
- Age: 35-65
- Profile: Busy professionals, dual-income families, successful entrepreneurs
- Values: Time over money, convenience, quality, technology

- **Pain Point:** "I don't have time to research, configure, and troubleshoot technology"
