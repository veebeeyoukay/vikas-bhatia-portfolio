# Creative Brief: Tampa Home Fix

## Business Overview

**Business Name:** Tampa Home Fix  
**Tagline:** Your Trusted Local Handyman  
**Industry:** Residential Handyman Services  
**Service Area:** Tampa Bay, Florida (Tampa, Brandon, Clearwater, St. Petersburg, surrounding areas)

---

## Brand Identity

### Brand Personality
- **Trustworthy** - Reliable, shows up on time, does quality work
- **Approachable** - Friendly, easy to talk to, no-nonsense
- **Competent** - Skilled, experienced, gets it right the first time
- **Local** - Part of the community, knows the area
- **Responsive** - Quick to respond, communicates clearly

### Brand Voice
- Friendly but professional
- Conversational, not corporate
- Confident without being boastful
- Uses plain language, avoids jargon
- Warm and helpful

### Brand Values
1. Quality workmanship
2. Honest pricing
3. Respect for your home
4. Clear communication
5. Timely service

---

## Visual Identity Guidelines

### Color Palette

**Primary Color:** Blue (#2563eb)
- Represents trust, reliability, professionalism
- Use for headers, buttons, primary CTAs

**Secondary Color:** Dark Blue (#1e40af)
- Use for accents, hover states, secondary elements

**Supporting Colors:**
- White (#ffffff) - Backgrounds, text on dark
- Light Gray (#f3f4f6) - Section backgrounds
- Dark Gray (#1f2937) - Body text
- Success Green (#10b981) - Confirmations, checkmarks
- Warning Orange (#f59e0b) - Highlights, badges

### Typography

**Headlines:** Bold, clean sans-serif
- Recommended: Inter, Poppins, or similar
- Weight: 700 (Bold)
- Size: 36-48px for main headlines

**Body Text:** Regular weight sans-serif
- Weight: 400-500
- Size: 16-18px
- Line height: 1.5-1.6

**Accent Text:** Medium weight
- Use for subheadlines, service names
- Weight: 500-600

### Logo Concepts

**Direction 1: Tool-Based Icon**
- Wrench or hammer icon integrated with house shape
- Clean, geometric style
- Single color works, two-color optional

**Direction 2: Wordmark + Icon**
- "Tampa Home Fix" in bold sans-serif
- Small tool or home icon as accent
- Stacked or horizontal layout options

**Direction 3: Badge/Seal Style**
- Circular or shield badge
- Conveys established, trustworthy
- Works well for uniforms, vehicle graphics

### Imagery Style

**Photography:**
- Real people, not stock photo perfect
- Working hands, tools in action
- Completed projects (before/after)
- Tampa area recognizable landmarks optional
- Diverse homeowners
- Well-lit, natural feeling

**Illustrations (if used):**
- Simple, line-based icons
- Consistent stroke weight
- Match primary blue color
- Home improvement themed

## Target Audience

### Primary: Busy Professionals (35-55)
- Dual-income households
- Limited time for DIY
- Value convenience over lowest price
- Own homes in Tampa suburbs
- Tech-comfortable, will book online

### Secondary: Seniors (60+)
- Need help with tasks they used to do themselves
- Value trustworthiness highly
- May prefer phone over online
- Often have ongoing project lists
- Referral-driven

### Tertiary: Property Managers
- Manage multiple rental properties
- Need reliable, responsive service
- Value consistency and communication
- Potential for recurring business

## Competitive Positioning

### Competitors
1. TaskRabbit / Handy (apps)
2. Local independent handymen
3. Big box store installation services (Home Depot, Lowe's)
4. General contractors (for larger jobs)

### Our Differentiators
1. **Local & Personal** - Not a faceless app, real local service
2. **Professional & Insured** - More reliable than Craigslist finds
3. **Right-Sized** - Not too big, not too small, just right for home repairs
4. **Communication** - Actually answers the phone, shows up on time
5. **Quality Focus** - Takes pride in work, not just checking boxes

### Positioning Statement
"For Tampa homeowners who want reliable handyman service without the hassle, Tampa Home Fix is the local handyman company that combines professional quality with personal service. Unlike impersonal apps or unreliable independents, we provide trustworthy, skilled handymen who communicate clearly and show up on time."
